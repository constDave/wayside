About Wayside

We're the bright yellow food truck behind the Back Lot bar. At Wayside, waffles aren't just for breakfast anymore. Our menu has something for everyone, from hand battered chicken, sweet carpese waffles, spicy buffalo sandwiches, and your new favorite burger. We are the waffle that you wanted, what all the other waffles promised.

Find us at 606 Maiden Ln next to Backlot bar and 1300 E. 6th at The Lucky Duck!
